# Classes & Character Creation

When you start a new game you'll create your character. This involves choosing a **name**, **class**, **background**, some starting **skills**, and your **appearance**.

---

## Classes

Your class defines your starting skill and the pool of skills you can unlock as you level up. Open the upgrade screen in-game with **Shift+C**.

### Martial Artist

> A master of hand-to-hand combat.

**Starting skill:** Hands Only — unarmed attacks deal more damage and cost half energy.

**Unlockable skills:**

| Skill | Effect |
|---|---|
| Roundhouse Kick | Strike all adjacent enemies at once (Key: K) |
| Sprinter | Move faster |
| Nimble | +5 AG |
| Combat Specialist | +5 HTCS |

---

### Sharpshooter

> An expert marksman with ranged weapons.

**Starting skill:** Deadshot — +10 CS on aimed shots; targeted aimed shots hit the chosen body part 90% of the time.

**Unlockable skills:**

| Skill | Effect |
|---|---|
| Sharpshooter | +5 CS |
| Brainy | +1 MA |
| Nimble | +5 AG |
| Ranged Combat Specialist | +5 CS |

---

### Combat Specialist

> Trained in the art of melee combat.

**Starting skill:** Brawler — +2 Natural SP, +1 PH.

**Unlockable skills:**

| Skill | Effect |
|---|---|
| Combat Specialist | +5 HTCS |
| Ranged Combat Specialist | +5 CS |
| Shove | Push the entity in front of you one tile (Key: V) |
| Hands Only | Unarmed attacks deal more damage and cost half energy |
| Sharpshooter | +5 CS |

---

## Backgrounds

Your background grants one starting skill on top of your class.

| Background | Granted Skill | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Rough Life | Brawler | +2 Natural SP, +1 PH |
| Athlete | Sprinter | Move faster |

---

## Stats

During character creation you also assign your core stats:

| Stat | What it does |
|---|---|
| PH (Physique) | Bare-hands penetration |
| AG (Agility) | Speed and coordination |
| MA (Mental Ability) | Intelligence |
| CL (Cool) | Composure; resists status effects like poison and slow |
| LD (Leadership) | Command presence |
| CS (Combat Skill) | Ranged attack accuracy (1–100 percentile) |
| HTCS (Hand-to-Hand CS) | Melee attack accuracy and parry (1–100 percentile) |
