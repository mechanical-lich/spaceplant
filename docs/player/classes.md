# Classes & Character Creation

When you start a new game you'll create your character. This involves choosing a **name**, **class**, **background**, some starting **skills**, and your **appearance**.

---

## Classes

Your class defines your stat bonuses, starting equipment, any class-specific skills, and the pool of skills you can unlock as you level up. Open the upgrade screen in-game with **Shift+C**.

---

### Scientist

> A researcher trained in advanced sciences. Sharp mind, steady hands.

**Stat bonuses:** +5 MA

**Starting equipment:** Laser Trimmers, Lab Coat, Scrubs, Scrub Shoes, Scrub Cap

**Unlockable skills:**

| Skill | Effect |
|---|---|
| Brainy | +1 MA |
| Nimble | +5 AG |
| Thick Skin | +2 Natural SP |
| Noncombustible | Half damage from fire |
| Acid Proof | Half damage from acid |

---

### Navigator

> Expert at reading star charts and getting everyone home in one piece.

**Stat bonuses:** +2 MA, +2 CL

**Starting equipment:** Standard Pistol, 9mm Rounds, Crew Shirt, Uniform Trousers, Crew Shoes, Crew Hat

**Starting skill:** Stellar Cartography — starts with the entire floor already revealed on the minimap.

**Unlockable skills:**

| Skill | Effect |
|---|---|
| Brainy | +1 MA |
| Nimble | +5 AG |
| Sprinter | Move faster |
| Sharpshooter | +5 CS |

### Officer

**Stat bonuses:** +3 LD, +2 PH

**Starting equipment:** Security Pistol, 9mm Rounds, Officer Shirt, Pressed Trousers, Officer Shoes, Officer Cap

**Starting skill:** Officer Clearance — rank grants access to most secured doors aboard the ship.

**Unlockable skills:**

|---|---|

| Brawler | +2 Natural SP, +1 PH |
| Thick Skin | +2 Natural SP |

## Backgrounds

Your background grants one starting skill on top of your class.

| Background | Granted Skill | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Rough Life | Brawler | +2 Natural SP, +1 PH |
| Athlete | Sprinter | Move faster |

---

## Stats

During character creation you also assign your core stats:

| Stat | What it does |
|---|---|
| PH (Physique) | Bare-hands penetration |
| AG (Agility) | Speed and coordination |
| MA (Mental Ability) | Intelligence |
| CL (Cool) | Composure; resists status effects like poison and slow |
| LD (Leadership) | Command presence |
| CS (Combat Skill) | Ranged attack accuracy (1–100 percentile) |
| HTCS (Hand-to-Hand CS) | Melee attack accuracy and parry (1–100 percentile) |
