# Backgrounds

A background is chosen during character creation and grants one starting skill. It represents your character's history before arriving on the station.

Backgrounds are separate from your class — you pick one of each. The class governs your stats and equipment; the background governs your starting knowledge and agenda.

---

## Saboteur

> "Hired to ensure this station never reports home. You know how to disappear."

**Starting skill:** `Saboteur Instinct`

Binds the **Place Mother Plant** action to `X`. You carry a mother plant cutting. Placing it introduces the infestation — the station's existing plant life will not start from a rooted mother plant at the beginning of your run. You are the source.

Your win condition is to place the cutting and then escape via life pod. See [Win Conditions](win_conditions.md) for the full Saboteur path.
